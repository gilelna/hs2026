My pairing recommendations

Berry #8B3A52 — pairs beautifully with your gold and warm-cream. Use for testimonial section backgrounds, week labels, or accent headings on light sections. Warm but more sophisticated than peach.

Terracotta #C4622D — use sparingly as a high-energy CTA accent. Works next to gold and espresso. Not a background color — better as a button or icon highlight on off-white or cream.

Deep Teal #1E5E5B — richer than your current green-dark (#026360) but very close. Consider using it instead as it's slightly warmer. Works as dark section bg with peach or gold text.

Slate #4A6E7A — a muted mid-tone between soft-blue and green-dark. Perfect for week/label badges, icon backgrounds, and secondary card bgs. Adds depth to the current palette.
